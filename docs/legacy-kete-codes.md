## Operator-as-platform

Business, professional services, and technology customers do not get
a pre-built industry kete. They subscribe to **Operator** at the same
$1,490/mo + $590 setup price and build their own workflows on top of
Iho (the governed router) plus the cross-cutting agents (AROHA,
SIGNAL, SENTINEL). Marketed at `/platform`.
