## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Assembl is New Zealand's first business intelligence platform, giving small and medium businesses access to the same quality of compliance, finance, marketing, HR, and operations support that large corporates take for granted. The platform provides 43 specialist tools across 16 industries, all built on current New Zealand legislation and accessible from a single subscription starting at $89 per month. Assembl is live at assembl.co.nz, fully operational, and preparing for its first revenue-generating campaign this week.

Assembl was founded by Kate Hudson, based in Auckland, New Zealand.

---

## THE PROBLEM

New Zealand has over 530,000 businesses. 97% of them are small or medium enterprises. These businesses face increasing regulatory complexity — there have been 47 significant law changes in the past 12 months alone — but most cannot afford the specialist advisors needed to stay compliant and competitive.

Large corporates have dedicated legal, HR, finance, and compliance departments. Small businesses have Google.

The average NZ SME spends between $15,000 and $50,000 per year on professional services such as lawyers, accountants, HR consultants, and compliance advisors. Many simply go without, exposing themselves to risk.

---

Assembl provides 43 industry-specific specialist tools covering 16 industries:

**Cost comparison:** Assembl starts at $89/month. The equivalent professional services cost $15,000 or more per year.

1. **NZ-specific:** The only platform built on current New Zealand legislation, not imported US or UK content.
