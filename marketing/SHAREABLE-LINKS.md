## Link Formats

- **Direct chat:** `https://assembl.co.nz/chat/[slug]`
- **WhatsApp share:** `https://wa.me/?text=[URL-encoded message]`
- **QR codes:** Use any QR generator (e.g., qr-code-generator.com) with the direct chat URL

### QR Code Generation Instructions
1. Go to [qr-code-generator.com](https://www.qr-code-generator.com/) (or any free QR tool)
2. Paste the agent's direct URL: `https://assembl.co.nz/chat/[slug]`
3. Download as PNG (300x300px minimum for print, 150x150px for digital)
4. Optional: Add the Assembl logo to the centre of the QR code for branding
