## 1. LinkedIn Launch Announcement (Long Form)

```
We just launched Assembl — and I couldn't be more proud to share it.

Assembl is the first AI operating system built specifically for New Zealand business.

Here's the problem we're solving:

NZ businesses have been using overseas AI tools that don't understand our legislation, our compliance requirements, or the way Kiwi businesses actually work. Ask ChatGPT about the Holidays Act, the Residential Tenancies Act, or NZ building consent — and you'll get answers written for a different country.

That's not good enough for a nation of 530,000+ businesses.

So we built something better.

Assembl gives every NZ business access to 42 AI agents — each a deep specialist in a real industry:

- APEX for construction compliance and tenders
- AURA for luxury hospitality operations
- HAVEN for property management and Healthy Homes
- ANCHOR for legal advice in plain English
- AROHA for HR and employment law
- LEDGER for accounting and GST
- COMPASS for immigration pathways
- TERRA for agriculture and freshwater compliance
- And 34 more across maritime, education, energy, retail, nonprofits, government, and beyond.

Every agent is grounded in NZ legislation — over 50 Acts of Parliament embedded with proactive compliance alerts.

We also built SPARK, a no-code AI app builder that lets you create quote calculators, client intake forms, and dashboards in seconds. No developer needed.

Pricing starts at $89/mo NZD for small businesses. Every agent offers three free messages — no credit card required. Try before you buy.

I built Assembl because I believe NZ businesses deserve world-class AI tools designed for us — not adapted from somewhere else. Based in Auckland, bootstrapped, and built with purpose.

If you run a NZ business — or know someone who does — I'd love for you to try it.

assembl.co.nz

#AssemblNZ #AIforNZ #NZBusiness #NZTech #StartupNZ #MadeInNZ
```
