## CANVA DESIGN NOTES

To create these in Canva:
- Use **1080x1080px** Instagram square format
- Dark background: **#0A0A14**
- Primary text: **#FAFAFA** (white)
- Accent text: **#00E5A0** (neon green)
- Secondary accent: **#00E5FF** (cyan) and **#B388FF** (purple)
- Font: **General Sans Bold** for headlines (or Space Grotesk Bold if unavailable)
- Body font: **Inter Regular**
- Add Assembl molecule logo (purple/cyan/green triangle) to corner of every slide
- Use glassmorphism effect on cards: semi-transparent backgrounds with blur
- Consistent slide numbering dots at bottom of each slide
