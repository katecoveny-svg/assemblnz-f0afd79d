## General Notes for All Templates

### Before sending:
- [ ] Personalise the opening — reference a recent article, episode, or initiative
- [ ] Verify the contact's current role and outlet
- [ ] Check the email address is correct (test with a small batch first)
- [ ] Ensure all links work (assembl.co.nz and agent-specific pages)
- [ ] Remove all [BRACKETED] placeholders
- [ ] Proofread for NZ English (organise not organize, licence not license, etc.)

### Follow-up schedule:
- **First follow-up:** 3-5 business days after initial email
- **Second follow-up:** 7-10 business days after first follow-up (only for Tier 1)
- **After that:** Move on. Don't pester. Revisit when you have a newsworthy update.

### CTA best practices:
- Always end with a specific, low-commitment ask ("Would a 15-minute call work?")
- Give them an easy out ("No worries if the timing isn't right")
- Make it easy to say yes — offer multiple formats (call, email Q&A, screen share)

### Tracking:
Log every outreach in your CRM or spreadsheet:
- Contact name, outlet, email
- Template used, date sent
- Opens and replies
- Follow-up dates and outcomes
- Coverage secured (with links)
