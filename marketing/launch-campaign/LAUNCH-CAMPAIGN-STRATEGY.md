**Campaign Owner:** Assembl (assembl.nz)

## 1. Campaign Overview

### The Big Idea

Every NZ business owner wears too many hats. They're the marketer, the HR manager, the accountant, the compliance officer, the IT department, the legal reviewer. They're juggling six subscriptions, four spreadsheets, and a growing sense that they're always behind.

**"Meet Your New Team"** introduces Assembl as the answer: 42 AI agents, each a specialist, working together as one brain. It is not another chatbot. It is not another SaaS tool. It is an AI operating system built specifically for New Zealand business.

### Campaign Name

**"Meet Your New Team"**

Subheadings by week:
- Week 1: "42 Agents. One Brain."
- Week 2: "Meet the Specialists"
- Week 3: "Real Businesses. Real Results."
- Week 4: "Your Team Is Ready"

### The Narrative Arc

| Week | Theme | Emotion | Action |
|------|-------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Awareness | Curiosity + recognition | "That's me — I'm doing all of that" |
| 2 | Education | Surprise + intrigue | "Wait, there's an AI agent for THAT?" |
| 3 | Social Proof | Trust + aspiration | "If it works for them, it could work for me" |
| 4 | Conversion | Urgency + confidence | "I'm in. Let me try this." |

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "The Stretched Operator"

- **Demographics:** NZ SME owner or senior operator, aged 30-55
- **Location:** Urban and regional NZ (Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch, Hamilton, Tauranga + regional towns)
- **Business size:** 1-50 employees
- **Digital behaviour:** Active on LinkedIn, uses Instagram for business inspiration, checks NZ Herald/Stuff daily

- $1,200 Meta budget allocation (higher volume, lower CPM)
- Target: 35-55 NZ SME owners, local business groups
- Formats: Video ads, carousel ads, link posts
- Goal: 150 signups from Facebook

**X/Twitter**
- 2 tweets per day (organic only, no paid)
- Target: Tech-savvy NZ founders, early adopters, media/journalists
- Formats: Thread launches, quote tweets, hot takes on NZ business/AI
- Goal: 30 signups + media pickup

**Google Ads**
- Search: Brand terms + competitor terms + intent keywords
- Display: Retargeting only (site visitors who didn't convert)
- $1,500 budget split: $1,000 Search / $500 Display retargeting

**PR & Earned Media**

- Target publications: NZ Herald Business, Stuff Business, NBR, interest.co.nz, Idealog, TechBlog.nz, The Spinoff

## 7. Tracking & Attribution

### UTM Convention

All campaign links follow this structure:

```
https://assembl.nz/?utm_source={platform}&utm_medium={type}&utm_campaign=meet-your-new-team&utm_content={asset-name}
```

**Examples:**
- `utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=meet-your-new-team&utm_content=founder-story`
- `utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=meet-your-new-team&utm_content=apex-spotlight`
- `utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=meet-your-new-team&utm_content=ai-business-nz`

### Tracking Tools

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Google Analytics 4 | Website traffic, conversion tracking, attribution |
| Meta Pixel | Facebook/Instagram ad conversion tracking |
| LinkedIn Insight Tag | LinkedIn ad tracking + audience building |
| Google Tag Manager | Centralised tag management |
| Stripe Dashboard | Revenue tracking, subscription analytics |
| Supabase Analytics | Product signups, trial activations, agent usage |

### Conversion Events to Track

1. **Page view** -- any page on assembl.nz
2. **Signup started** -- clicked "Get Started" or "Start Free Trial"
3. **Signup completed** -- account created
4. **Trial activated** -- first agent interaction
5. **Paid conversion** -- Stripe checkout completed
6. **Agent selected** -- which agent they chose first (intent signal)

## 8. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Low initial engagement | Medium | High | Front-load budget in Week 1; A/B test ad creative in first 48 hours |
| High CPA on LinkedIn | High | Medium | Cap LinkedIn daily spend; shift budget to Meta if CPA exceeds $25 |
| Media doesn't pick up PR | Medium | Low | Direct outreach to 3 priority journalists; offer exclusive angles |
| Trial-to-paid conversion below 10% | Medium | High | Activate email nurture sequence; add in-app onboarding prompts |
| Negative sentiment / "AI replacing jobs" | Low | High | Prepare response framework; position as "AI that works WITH you" |
| Budget exhaustion before Week 4 | Low | High | Daily budget monitoring; pause underperforming campaigns by Day 10 |

## 9. Post-Campaign Transition

After Week 4, transition to evergreen content strategy:

1. **Top-performing ads** become always-on campaigns with reduced budget
2. **Agent spotlight series** continues as weekly content pillar
3. **Email sequences** run perpetually for new signups
4. **Retargeting** remains active for all website visitors
5. **Monthly campaign review** to optimise based on 4-week learnings

### Success Criteria for "Always-On" Qualification

An ad or content piece qualifies for evergreen status if it achieves:
- CPA below $15 NZD
- CTR above 2% (social) or 5% (search)
- Engagement rate above 3% (organic social)
