### LinkedIn NZ Business Influencers

Target NZ-based LinkedIn creators who post about:
- NZ tech and startups
- SME business operations
- AI and automation
- NZ entrepreneurship

**Outreach approach:**
1. Engage with their content for 1-2 weeks before pitching
2. Send a personalised LinkedIn message (not a connection request with pitch)
3. Offer early access to Assembl in exchange for an honest review/post
4. Provide them with key stats and talking points they can use

### NZ TikTok/Instagram Business Creators

Target NZ creators in niches that align with Assembl's agents:
- Small business tips / NZ business life
- Hospitality content creators
- Tradie/construction content
- NZ farming and rural life
- Accounting and tax tips for NZ

**Outreach approach:**
1. DM with a short, casual pitch — "Built an AI tool specifically for NZ businesses, would love your take"
2. Offer free access in exchange for a review or mention
3. Create a short demo video they can react to or duet
4. Provide a unique referral code for tracking

## Outreach Priority & Sequencing

| Phase | Timing | Targets | Action |
|-------|--------|---------|--------|
| Pre-launch exclusives | 1 week before launch | 1-2 Tier 1 outlets (NZ Herald or NBR) | Offer embargoed early access |
| Launch day | Day of launch | All Tier 1 + Tier 2 | Send press release + tailored pitch |
| Week 1 follow-up | Days 2-5 | Non-responsive Tier 1 + Tier 2 | Follow-up email |
| Industry outreach | Week 2 | All Tier 3 | Industry-specific pitches |
| Podcast pitching | Weeks 2-3 | Tier 4 podcasts | Guest pitch emails |
| Influencer outreach | Weeks 3-4 | Tier 4 influencers | LinkedIn DMs + social engagement |
