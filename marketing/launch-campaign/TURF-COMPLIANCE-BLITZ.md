# TURF Compliance Crisis Campaign
## "10 Days to Save Your Club" — Marketing Blitz Strategy

**Campaign window:** 26 March – 5 April 2026 (10 days)
**Core message:** Thousands of NZ sports clubs face automatic dissolution on 5 April. TURF generates compliant constitutions in minutes — for free.
**Founder:** Kate Hudson, Assembl | assembl.co.nz

---

## THE CRISIS (talking points)

- **Incorporated Societies Act 2022** requires all 24,000+ incorporated societies to re-register by **5 April 2026**
- Clubs that miss the deadline are **automatically dissolved** — bank accounts frozen, insurance voided, facility leases cancelled
- Volunteer numbers have **halved since 2019** (31 → 18 average per club)
- Two-thirds of community clubs are **losing money or breaking even**
- The Act mirrors the **Companies Act** — corporate-grade governance requirements applied to volunteer organisations
- Most clubs **cannot afford legal counsel** to draft compliant constitutions
- Regional Sports Trusts are overwhelmed — not enough capacity to reach every club

## WHAT TURF DOES (unique selling points)

- Generates a **complete 14-section compliant constitution** in minutes
- Covers **18+ sporting codes** with sport-specific purpose clauses (rugby, cricket, football, netball, hockey, tennis, basketball, swimming, athletics, bowls, golf, surf life saving, rowing, sailing, rugby league, touch, cycling, multi-sport)
- **6-step guided re-registration walkthrough**
- Code-specific **affiliation clauses** (NZ Rugby, NZC, FNZ, NNZ, HNZ)
- Built on **Sport NZ Governance Code** principles
- **Charity compliance** — dual Incorporated Societies Act + Charities Act 2005
- **Grant application drafting** for NZCT, Lion Foundation, Pub Charity, NZ Lotteries
- Officer duty statements, conflict of interest policies, dispute resolution procedures
- Financial reporting frameworks aligned with the Act
- Voice-enabled — clubs can talk through the process hands-free
