## 6. A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1-2: Test creative angles

| Test | Variant A | Variant B |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Hook | Pain point ("6 subscriptions") | Aspiration ("42 specialists") |
| Offer frame | Price-led ("From $89/mo") | Value-led ("Replace $15K/mo") |
| CTA | "Start free trial" | "Meet your new team" |
| Creative | Static image | Video (15 sec) |

### Week 3-4: Scale winners, test offers

| Test | Variant A | Variant B |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Offer | "Free trial" | "50% off first month" |
| Urgency | No deadline | "Ends Sunday" |
| Social proof | No testimonial | With testimonial quote |
| Agent focus | General (42 agents) | Specific (APEX or HELM) |

### Decision criteria
- Pause any ad with CPA above $25 NZD after 500 impressions
- Scale any ad with CPA below $12 NZD
- Minimum 48 hours before making optimisation decisions
- Shift budget from underperforming channels to top performers weekly
