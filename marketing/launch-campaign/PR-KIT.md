## 8. FAQ for Journalists

**Q: Is Assembl just ChatGPT with a NZ wrapper?**
A: No. While Assembl uses large language models as part of its infrastructure, every agent has been built with NZ-specific training data, legislation references, and industry context. The agents don't just answer questions -- they generate NZ-compliant documents, run compliance audits, and produce industry-specific outputs that reference current NZ law.

**Q: How does Assembl stay current with NZ legislation changes?**
A: The platform's legislation references are updated on an ongoing basis. When NZ law changes -- minimum wage updates, new compliance requirements, regulatory amendments -- the relevant agents are updated to reflect those changes.

**Q: Where is user data stored?**
A: Assembl is committed to data privacy and complies with the Privacy Act 2020. User data is not used for model training.

**Q: Does Assembl replace lawyers, accountants, or consultants?**
A: No. Assembl provides first drafts, compliance checks, and starting points. For complex legal, financial, or compliance matters, users should always consult qualified professionals. Assembl reduces the need for routine consultations and helps users arrive better-prepared when they do engage specialists.

**Q: Who is Assembl for?**
A: Primarily NZ small and medium businesses (1-50 employees), but also includes personal/family tools (HELM) and industry-specific agents for larger organisations. The platform spans sole traders to enterprises.

**Q: How does the pricing work?**
A: Plans range from $29/mo (HELM family agent) to $599/mo (all 42 agents). A free trial is available on all plans. Enterprise pricing is custom. All prices are in NZD and include GST.
